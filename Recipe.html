INGREDIENTS

  • All purpose flour
  • Butter,softened
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Semi chocolate chips
  • Baking powder

baking intructions

    Making cholocalate chip cookies is a fairly simple process. Here are the general guide:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients like flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Using a mixer, cream together softened butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Fold in your chocolate chips with a spatula.
  6. Scoop dough onto prepared baking sheets, leaving space for spreading
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  8. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
